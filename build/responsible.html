Do you have a problem?

Do you take an interest to gambling games? Are you worried that you depend on gaming? Or you just want to play safe and give a try yourself? We suggest you to try yourself with the test that will help to prove does the game have bad influence on your life.

Test is a simple questionnaire. Your task is to answer “Yes” or “No”. If you will get a high number of “Yes”, then it means that gaming is a serious difficulty for you.

Test questions:

  • What is the aim of your game: kill routine or leisure time?
  • You play all alone?
  • Does the gaming have an effect to you day duty (study, work, etc.)?
  • Somebody criticize you for the gaming?
  • Do you have any necessity to play more after bad luck or quarrel with people in your life?
  • You have less concern to the world around, friends or family, family pet or drop the ball on your hobbies?
  • You take hard when you need to spend your “money for game” for something else?
  • Did you ever lie that you have had a real good time in game?
  • Have you ever keep close real amount of spent money?
  • Have you ever lie, thieve or incur debt for gaming?
  • Do you loss all your money at a time?
  • When you have a bad luck, do you have a desire to play again and refund money?
  • Do you want to re-enter the game fast when spent all money in the process of the game?
  • Do you have a depression because of game?
  • Do you have any thoughts about suicide?
